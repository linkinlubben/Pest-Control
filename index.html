How it works

Four steps to a protected perimeter

A real process, not a mystery spray. Here's exactly what happens from your first call.

1

Inspect

We walk your property, find entry points and activity, and map where pests are getting in.

2

Treat

We treat inside hot spots and knock down what's already active — targeted, not blanketed.

3

Barrier

We lay a protective perimeter around the foundation so crawling pests can't cross back in.

4

Maintain

We return on schedule to keep the barrier active — and any time in between, free of charge.

The Perimeter Promise

See a pest between visits? We're back — no charge.

Your plan isn't finished until the pests are gone. If anything shows up between scheduled treatments, we return and re-treat at no additional cost. Every product we use is applied to be safe around kids and pets.
