Good to know

Questions before you book

How soon can you come out?
Most first visits happen within the same week. When you book online you'll choose a day and an arrival window that works for you.
Do I need to be home for the treatment?
For the first visit it helps so we can walk the property with you and map entry points. Ongoing exterior treatments can be done without you home — we'll leave a summary of what we found and treated.
Are the treatments safe around children and pets?
Yes. Every product is applied by a licensed technician following label guidance to be safe around your family. We'll let you know if any area needs a short dry time.
What if pests come back between visits?
That's what the guarantee is for. Reach out any time and we'll return to re-treat at no additional charge — your plan isn't done until the pests are gone.
Can I switch or combine plans later?
Absolutely. Many homes pair Home Protection with seasonal Yard Enjoyment, and add Termite Defense once. Tell us what you're seeing and we'll tailor it.
