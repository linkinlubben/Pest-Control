About Perimeter

The crew that draws the line — and holds it.

Perimeter started with one idea: most pest problems are really boundary problems. Seal the edges, treat what's active, and keep the barrier live all year. That's still exactly how we work.

Founded in 2009 by a technician who was tired of one-and-done sprays that didn't last.

Our founder spent years watching companies treat the symptom and leave. Perimeter was built to do the opposite — map how pests actually get in, close those routes, and stand behind the work with a guarantee that means something.

Today we're a local team of licensed technicians protecting thousands of homes across the region. We're small enough to know our customers by name and disciplined enough to show up on schedule, every season.

No lock-in contracts. No mystery fees. Just a protected perimeter and a promise to come back until the pests are gone.
