1. Information we collect

Information you give us. When you book a visit, use the contact form, or apply for a job, we collect what you enter — typically your name, email, phone number, service address, and any details you share about your property or request.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we may collect standard technical data such as your IP address, browser and device type, pages viewed, and referring links, through cookies and similar technologies (see the Cookie Notice).

2. How we use your information

  • Schedule, provide, and follow up on pest control services

  • Honor our service guarantee and keep service records
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience

  • Meet legal, tax, and safety obligations

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with:

  • Service providers who help us operate — for example scheduling, payment, email, or analytics vendors — under agreements that limit their use of it

4. Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to run the site, remember preferences, and understand how it's used. You can control these through your browser and, where offered, our cookie controls. See our Cookie Notice for details.

5. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above, to run our business, and to meet legal requirements — then we delete or anonymize it.

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your information, or to object to or restrict certain processing.
